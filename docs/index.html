Queen: Reigning Supreme in Rock History

Queen, the legendary British rock band formed in 1970, is known for their unforgettable songs and captivating performances. Led by the iconic Freddie Mercury, they created timeless hits like "Bohemian Rhapsody" and "We Will Rock You," earning a place in music history.
